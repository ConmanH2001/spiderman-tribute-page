Description

Spiderman is a superhero whos powers are based on that of a spider. He has a red and blue outfit and his real name is Peter Parker. He lives with his aunt may and his uncle ben, and depending on the universe, he gets into a relationship with Mary Jane Watson, Gwen Stacy, Liz Allen, or Black Cat.

Powers

Spiderman has the proportional strength of a spider, which is basically just super strength. He also has his spider sense, which is a 6th sense that alerts hi when danger is approaching around him. He also has enhanced speed, enhanced reflexes, and can walk on walls. Even before he was bitten by a spider, Spiderman still was incredibly smart and created his web shooters and web fluid to give him the ability to shoot webs like a spider.

Backstory

Peter Parker was an average nerdy teenager who one day got bitten by a spider. That spider was radioactive and he gained the abilits of a spider which he used for street fighting for money. One day when he was walking he witnessed a robbery, the man ran past him and Peter let him run on by, thats when the robber shot his uncle and stole his car. Peter held his Uncle ben in his arms as he whispered his final words: "with great power comes great responsibility." This became a drivng force for peter in becoming spiderman and never turning away from helping someone else again.

Villians

Spiderman has a large plethora of villians, such as Doctor Octopus, Sandman, Green Goblin, Hob Goblin, Venom, Kraven the Hunter, Shocker, Mysterio, Vulture, and many many more! All have a different array of powers and each of them able to beat spiderman in their own ways, but with spiderman's spidey sense, vast intellect, enhanced strength, speed, and reflexes and quick thinking, he will almost always come out on top.
